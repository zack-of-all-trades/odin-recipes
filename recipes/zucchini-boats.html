Zucchini Boats

Italian Sausage Stuffed Zucchini Boats

Summer squash season is just around the corner, and as usual, zucchini is the leader of the pack. This sweet, summertime squash makes a wonderful appetizer or side dish, but how to make it a meal? Stuff it! Zucchini boats are the perfect way to make an otherwise light veggie side into a hearty and filling meal.

To make zucchini boats, simply scoop the flesh out of the zucchini halves and stuff them with a filling of your choice. Voila! You have a meal, and a creative way to use up all the zucchini in your garden. Not to mention it's a low-carb alternative to your favorite meaty and cheesy dishes.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchini
  • 1lb italian sausage
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 mushrooms, finely diced
  • 1/2 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 slices bread, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 8 slices pepper jack cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
